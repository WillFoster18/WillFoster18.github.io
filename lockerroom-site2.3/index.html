The AI Fit Confidence engine

Know it fits before it ships.

Racers waste money guessing. Enter your profile once — discipline, height, weight, boot last, suit measurements — and every listing shows a Fit Confidence score built for your body, not a generic size chart.

How the score is built

Fit Confidence blends the seller's listed specs against your racer profile and gear-specific fit data — not just S/M/L.

  • 01Build your racer profile once — height, weight, discipline, boot last width, current gear.
  • 02Our model compares it against the listing's specs: flex, radius, boot volume, suit cut, wear level.
  • 03You get a single confidence score plus a plain-language explanation of any risk — not a black box.
